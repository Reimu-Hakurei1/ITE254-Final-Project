Meet the Characters

Mario

Personality & Role: Mario is courageous, joyful, and endlessly optimistic. When danger strikes, he leaps first and thinks later, always with a smile and a sense of justice. As the Mushroom Kingdom’s hero, he has saved Princess Peach countless times, stood up to Bowser and his minions, and inspires other heroes by example.

Likes & Hobbies: Racing karts, exploring new worlds, collecting power-ups, helping friends, and celebrating every small victory.

Voice Lines:

Luigi

Personality & Role: Luigi may seem timid and shy, but he is also deeply loyal, kindhearted, and always ready to step up when it matters most. Though he often lives in Mario’s shadow, his courage shines through—especially when he helps his brother or faces his fears head-on.

Likes & Hobbies: Exploring haunted mansions (despite being scared), experimenting with gadgets, spending time with his brother, and secretly wanting to prove himself as a hero in his own right.

Voice Lines:

Toad

Personality & Role: Toad is cheerful, humble, and full of energy. Even though he sometimes gets scared in the face of danger, he refuses to give up. He’s always ready to help his friends and puts the Mushroom Kingdom first, no matter how small he might feel.

Likes & Hobbies: Mushrooms, assisting Mario, organizing events, adventures (like Mario Kart or the many parties), and making people smile.

Voice Lines:

Reimu Hakurei

Personality & Role: A shrine maiden called to safeguard the barrier between the human world and the youkai realm, Reimu is cool, calm, and straightforward. She often appears laid-back—sometimes even lazy—but in critical times she shows tremendous skill, strength, and willpower. She faces supernatural threats with determination, even when she dislikes unnecessary effort.

Likes & Hobbies: Peaceful solitude, shrine maintenance, floating study breaks, enjoying simple pleasures, and occasionally letting out her spiritual power when the boundary between worlds is disturbed.
